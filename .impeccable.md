## Design Context

### Users
Internal team at Dhīmahi Technolabs — presales, solutions, and consulting staff who manage AI use cases and demo readiness. They use this tool daily to track what demos exist, their status, costs, and access instructions. They're tech-savvy professionals who value speed and clarity over aesthetics. The tool needs to get out of their way.

### Brand Personality
**Sharp, efficient, no-nonsense.**
This is an internal ops tool, not a marketing surface. It should feel like something a competent team built for themselves — no unnecessary chrome, no decorative flourishes. Every element earns its place by communicating information or enabling action.

Emotional goal: *confidence and control* — users should always know exactly where they are and what to do next.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Reference: Linear** — tight typography, high information density, restrained color use, purposeful whitespace. Dark and light modes both supported.

**Brand colors from dhimahitechnolabs.com:**
- Primary: `#215b6f` (deep teal — used for CTAs, active states, key UI chrome)
- Primary hover/dark: `#1a4a5a`
- Accent/secondary: `#7cc0ba` (soft teal — used for highlights, status indicators, secondary actions)
- Soft background tint: `#e8f5f3`

**Light mode:** Near-white backgrounds (`#f8fafc`), dark text (`#0f172a`), teal primary.
**Dark mode:** Deep dark backgrounds (`#0d1117` or `#111827`), light text, teal primary remains consistent.

**Anti-references:** No Bootstrap defaults. No generic SaaS blue (avoid `#3b82f6` as primary). No card-heavy dashboard clichés. No gradients for the sake of gradients. No emoji in UI labels.

**Typography:** System-ui or Inter — clean, legible, slightly tight letter-spacing for headings. Functional labels are small and subdued. Data is prominent.

### Design Principles

1. **Data first** — The most important information (status, count, title) is immediately visible without interaction. No decorative containers that bury content.

2. **Restrained color** — Teal is used sparingly and intentionally: active states, status badges, primary actions. Everything else is neutral grays. Color signals meaning, not decoration.

3. **Density with breathing room** — Tables and lists over card grids where appropriate. Compact rows. Generous page margins. Never claustrophobic, never airy-for-the-sake-of-it.

4. **Light and dark parity** — Both modes are first-class. Neither is an afterthought. The same hierarchy and contrast ratios hold in both themes.

5. **Zero friction interactions** — Forms are fast to fill. Buttons are obviously primary vs secondary. Error states are clear without being alarming. No confirmation dialogs for low-stakes actions.
